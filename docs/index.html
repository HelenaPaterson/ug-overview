Chapter 1 Introduction

This book presents an overview of our undergraduate practical Teaching in Psychology using R and the Tidyverse for reproducible data analysis and open educational practices for developing good research practices. We highlight briefly what we have done in developing our practcal methodology and analysis classes; and give some examples that can be adapted to different teaching settings. We then link to resources in the different parts of our curriculum and assessment.

1.1 Who we are

We are a growing team of teachers in the School of Psychology and the Institute of Neuroscience and Psychology at the University of Glasgow who collectively call ourselves #PsyTeachR. We have lead the way in changing teaching of analysis practical skills in our undergraduate and postgraduate programmes since 2014.
